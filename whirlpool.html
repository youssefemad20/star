Whirlpool (M51)

The Perfect Spiral

Basic Identity

Type Grand-design Spiral
Distance 23M LY
Interaction Active

The Whirlpool Galaxy is famous because it looks like a textbook perfect spiral galaxy.

Why It Looks Perfect

Its clear, symmetrical, and bright arms are caused by gravitational interaction with a smaller neighboring galaxy.

  • Gravity compresses gas.
  • Triggers star birth.
  • Enhances spiral structure.

Scientific Importance

M51 was the first galaxy where spiral structure was recognized. It teaches us about:

  • Spiral arm formation.
  • Tidal forces.
  • Lesson: Spiral arms are density waves (like traffic jams), not solid structures.

Big Insight

The Whirlpool Galaxy proves that gravity shapes beauty in the universe.
