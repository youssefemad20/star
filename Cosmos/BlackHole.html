Who Found Them?

Karl Schwarzschild first thought of them in 1916 using Einstein's math. The first real candidate, Cygnus X-1, was found in 1971. In 2019, we finally took the first-ever photo of one!

How are they born?

When a massive star dies, it collapses under its own weight. If the star is big enough, it crushes itself down into a tiny point with infinite gravity!
