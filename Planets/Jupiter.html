The Great Red Spot

Jupiter has a giant storm called the "Great Red Spot" that has been raging for hundreds of years. This storm is so massive that the entire Earth could fit inside it! The winds there blow faster than any hurricane on Earth.

A Mini Solar System?

Jupiter has so many moons (over 90!) that it's like its own mini solar system. The four biggest ones are called Galilean moons: Io (full of volcanoes), Europa (an ice world with an ocean underneath), Ganymede (the biggest moon ever), and Callisto.

Vacuum Cleaner of Space

Because Jupiter is so massive, it has super strong gravity. This gravity helps protect Earth by pulling in or deflecting dangerous asteroids and comets that might otherwise hit us. Thanks, giant neighbor!
