Saturn

The Lord of the Rings

Saturn is famous for its beautiful, dazzling rings. It's the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest gas giant. It's a majestic world of yellow and gold clouds, but those rings steal the show! Adorned with a dazzling, complex system of icy rings, Saturn is unique in our solar system. The other giant planets have rings, but none are as spectacular as Saturn's.

Scientific Deep Dive

What are the Rings made of?

From far away, the rings look solid like a frisbee. But up close, they are actually made of billions of chunks of ice and rock! Some are as small as grains of sand, while others are as big as a house or even a mountain.

It Could Float!

Saturn is huge but very "fluffy" (low density). It's the only planet in our solar system that is less dense than water. If you could find a bathtub big enough to hold it, Saturn would actually float like a rubber ducky!

Titan: An Amazing Moon
